Community Involvement

We believe businesses have a responsibility to give back. Here's how we show up.

📚

Little Free Library Program

We sponsor and stock 12 Little Free Libraries across the city, donating over 200 books per month to ensure everyone in our community has access to reading materials.

🏫

School Literacy Partnerships

Book Haven partners with 8 local schools to run reading clubs, author visits, and book drives. Last year we donated $15,000 in books to Title I classrooms.

🍞

Charity Bake Sale & Book Drive

Every November we host our annual Bake Sale + Book Drive, raising funds and collecting used books for local shelters and community centers. Last year: 1,200 books collected.

🤝

Refugee & New Arrival Support

We partner with local resettlement organizations to provide free books, language-learning resources, and ESL dictionaries to newly arrived community members.
