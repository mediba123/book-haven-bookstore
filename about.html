About Book Haven

Where every book tells a story — and every customer becomes part of ours.

Our Story

Book Haven Bookstore was founded in 2010 by lifelong reader and former librarian Margaret Ellis, who dreamed of a neighborhood bookstore that felt like a second home. Nestled in a restored Victorian building on Maple Street, Book Haven has served our community for over a decade with warmth, expertise, and a genuinely curated collection.

We believe books change lives — and that the right book at the right moment can open worlds. That's why our staff reads voraciously and takes your reading recommendations personally. Whether you're looking for a gift, a new obsession, or a rare collectible, we'll help you find it.

Our mission is simple: to be the most welcoming, knowledgeable, and community-rooted bookstore in the region. We are proud supporters of local literacy programs, school partnerships, and the simple joy of sitting down with a great book.
